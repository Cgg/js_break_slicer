JS Break Slicer Huh?

This small app randomly reorganizes drum breaks, cutting them along the drum beats. The first row shows the input (the break in its original form), and the second row shows the randomized break.

  • The vertical red lines figures the places where the algorithm thought there was a beat;
  • The rainbow stripes figure the slices out.
